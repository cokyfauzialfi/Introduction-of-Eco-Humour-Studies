Rhetorical Devices: Irony, exaggeration, metaphor.

"Humour Beyond Human": Transcending anthropocentrism. (Heidari-Shahreza)

Psychological Impact
Cognitive Lens: Increases attention & accessibility.

Emotional Regulation: Activates limbic system for tension relief. (Martin & Ford)
Politics & Ethics
"Comic Corrective": Makes societal change palatable.

Ethical Risks: Trivialization, greenwashing, or offense. (Navasky)
Humour Types
Constructive: Irony, playful, black humour.

Harmful: Aggressive, hurtful, or banal varieties.
Scope & Gaps
Exclusions: Generic humour or non-environmental movements.

Gaps: Lack of Non-Western perspectives & spontaneous interactional humour.
Academic Position
Marginalized: Peripheral to parent disciplines like Humour Studies.

Interdisciplinary: Bridges Natural Sciences & Humanities.
